About us

Our website connects farmers with agriculture experts who can mentor and solve problems of marginal farmers and their farm. This system also enables farmers and agronomists to view lists of fertilizers, pesticides, tools and government policies relating to farming. Farmers may ask questions related to their crops, use of fertilizers, pesticides and tools. Questions may include their crop name, problem description and question image. Farmers can also view their as well as other previously asked questions, and their respective answers.
